# Life Hug — Question Bank

Pass 1: Skeleton

---

## A: Origins (Childhood & Family)
- [ ] A1: What's your earliest memory?
- [ ] A2: Tell me about where you grew up. What did it look like, feel like, smell like?
- [ ] A3: What was your family's financial situation growing up? When did you first understand it?
- [ ] A4: Tell me about a time you moved to a new place or your world changed. What was the hardest part?
- [ ] A5: What did your parents teach you without meaning to?

## B: Becoming (Growing Up & Finding Direction)
- [ ] B1: When did you first feel like you had agency over your own life?
- [ ] B2: Tell me about your first job. What did it teach you?
- [ ] B3: Was there a person who changed the trajectory of your life? What happened?
- [ ] B4: What was the first big risk you took?
- [ ] B5: What's something you believed strongly when you were younger that you no longer believe?

## C: Relationships & People
- [ ] C1: Who's the person who believed in you before you believed in yourself?
- [ ] C2: Tell me about a friendship that shaped who you are.
- [ ] C3: How did your upbringing affect how you build relationships?
- [ ] C4: Tell me about someone you lost — or lost touch with — who still matters to you.
- [ ] C5: Who do you call when everything falls apart?

## D: Purpose & Calling
- [ ] D1: What moment made you decide to pursue the path you're on?
- [ ] D2: What were you doing before you found your calling? Why did you leave?
- [ ] D3: Tell me about the first conversation where your current path started to take shape.
- [ ] D4: What did people say when you told them your plan?
- [ ] D5: What keeps you going when it gets hard?

## E: Reflection & Wisdom
- [ ] E1: What's the most important lesson your life has taught you?
- [ ] E2: What would you tell your 18-year-old self?
- [ ] E3: How has your definition of success changed over time?
- [ ] E4: What are you most proud of that has nothing to do with work?
- [ ] E5: What do you want the people you love to know about your life?
