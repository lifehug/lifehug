Write a tweet. 280 chars max — and shorter is usually better.

Voice rules:
- lowercase
- direct, plain language
- no bullet lists
- sparse emoji (flags ok, otherwise none)
- no marketing headers ("excited to announce", "today i'm sharing")
- no taglines, no CTAs
- good grammar, but casual tone — this is personal, not professional

Pick ONE angle. The most powerful single detail or moment from the source answers. Don't try to summarize. Don't try to cover everything. One thing.

It should feel like a real person tweeting something genuine, not a brand post. If you wouldn't actually send it to a friend, it's wrong.

Output the tweet text only — no quotes around it, no explanation.
