Write a personal post.

This can be a blog post, longer social post, or public reflection. It should
feel authored, specific, and human rather than promotional.

Voice rules:
- Match the author's natural voice from the source material.
- Use concrete scenes and details before abstract reflection.
- Avoid generic life lessons unless the source material earns them.
- No hashtags, CTAs, or marketing framing unless specifically requested.

Structure:
- Open with a specific moment, image, or honest claim.
- Build through the relevant memory or relationship thread.
- Land on what the moment means now.

Length: 400-1000 words unless the source material clearly wants something
shorter.

Output the post only.
