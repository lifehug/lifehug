Write an Instagram caption.

Length: longer than a tweet but still concise. 2-4 short paragraphs max. If a single paragraph says it, use one.

Voice rules:
- Personal tone. Good grammar.
- 1-2 emoji maximum, only if they actually add something.
- No hashtags unless specifically requested.
- No "swipe to see more", no "link in bio", no influencer scaffolding.
- Should feel like a real person sharing something meaningful — not a brand post.

Structure options (pick what fits the source material):
- A single moment told vividly, then one line of reflection.
- A short story arc: setup, turn, landing.
- A direct address to one person, with a moment from the source as the anchor.

Quotes are welcome if a line from the source answers is powerful enough to stand on its own — but don't force one in.

Output the caption only.
